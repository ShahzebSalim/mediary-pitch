Solving the Cold Start Problem in Medical Tech

Mediary bypasses the adoption barrier through a Product-Led Growth flywheel that connects patients, doctors, and clinics seamlessly.

Executive Summary: The Cold Start Solution

🎯 The B2C Wedge

Patients use Mediary to instantly OCR-scan, translate, and organize messy physical reports into a clean digital timeline. Zero friction, maximum value.

🔗 The B2B Loop

When patients share their Pre-Visit Prep link with a new doctor, that doctor experiences Mediary's value instantly—saving 15 minutes of intake time—acting as a direct B2B inbound generator.

The Result: A self-reinforcing flywheel where B2C adoption drives B2B revenue, eliminating the traditional "chicken and egg" problem that plagues health-tech startups.

The Sales Pipeline & Support Architecture

Outbound to Demo Handoff: 3 Phases

Phase 1: The SDR Phase (The Wedge)

The outbound engine secures the meeting by promising to solve a specific administrative bottleneck.

Phase 2: The AE Phase (The "Aha!" Demo)

Account Executives perform "Live Ingestion Demos." The AE asks the Clinic Director for a redacted patient history, drops it into Mediary live, and allows the AI to organize it instantly.

Phase 3: Pilot Trial (Progetto Pilota)

Offer a 30-day trial limited to 1–2 "Champion Doctors" within the clinic. If success criteria are met, the annual contract triggers automatically.

Multi-Tiered Support Matrix

🏢 Tier 3: Mediary to B2B

Account Executives act as Customer Success Managers, providing VIP technical support and API integration assistance.

🤝 Tier 2: Clinic to B2C Patient

The clinic acts as the "Reseller," instructing patients on document uploads and handling basic usage questions.

👥 Tier 1: Mediary to B2C Patient

Direct B2C support via in-app AI chatbot with escalation to human ticketing system for major bugs.
